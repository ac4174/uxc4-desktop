Who am I?

AJC is a visual Designer, an styleanimator, a digital designer, a user experience designer and researcher who hungers for knowledge and creating beautiful multimedia art, design, and product with a humorous persona. He is a team-player eager to collaberating with others in delievering a well-detailed and meaningful craftsmanships to his audience. Very culturally interested in the field of gaming, comic books, tv-shows, animation to fantasy to sci-scifi movies. In his spare time, he creates storyboard art and concepts.
